ABOUT MAYONDO WOOD AND FURNITURE LTD
What Drives Us

MWF is a b2b wood and furniture company which outsources wood products such as timber,poles,hard and soft woods from upcountry suppliers and resales them to other companies and organizations such as building and construction companies as well as medium and large wood furniture dealers.

Shukran

Business Operations

MWF has a showroom for furniture products and a warehouse nearby for woods which is managed by 8 trained attendants to support the business by recording the stock and sales as well as loading and offloading. On recordinga sale.5% is usually added to the total price if the company is to provide transport to the customer.A periodic sales report is issued to the manager whenever it is required.

Reporting

All wood and furniture stock is recorded by the manager before they are available for sale.

Name of the product,type of the product,cost price,quantity,product price,supplier name,date,quality of the product among others are considered. Stock levels,cost price,product price of a particular product are subjected to changes/updates and a stock report is availed whenever it is required to the manager.
